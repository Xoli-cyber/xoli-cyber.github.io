About NDASWU

Empowering Workers. Advancing Justice.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision

To be a leading, inclusive, and transformative trade union that empowers and unites all workers across South Africa and beyond — advancing dignity, fairness, and justice in the world of work through solidarity, innovation, and servant leadership.

Mission

  • Promoting equitable and dignified working conditions for every worker, regardless of sector or background.
  • Strengthening worker unity through democratic participation, transparency, and ethical leadership.
  • Harnessing technology to provide efficient, modern, and accessible union services.
  • Building workers’ capacity through education, skills development, and continuous learning.
  • Advocating for progressive labour legislation and social transformation rooted in justice and fairness.
  • Extending solidarity to unorganised, informal, and gig economy workers to ensure that no worker is left behind.

Core Values

  • Solidarity: We stand united as workers across all sectors, recognising that our collective strength is our greatest power.
  • Democracy: We uphold participatory, transparent, and accountable decision-making at every level of the union.
  • Servant Leadership: We lead with humility, integrity, and a commitment to serve members before self.
  • Justice and Fairness: We fight for equity, equality, and respect for the rights and dignity of all workers.
  • Professionalism: We promote competence, reliability, and excellence in all our operations and engagements.
  • Inclusivity and Diversity: We value and actively promote gender equity, representation, and diversity in leadership and membership.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology and new ideas to modernise trade unionism and improve member services.
  • Collective Empowerment: We build worker consciousness through education, dialogue, and shared responsibility.
  • Accountability: We are answerable to our members and committed to transparency in all actions and resources.
  • International Solidarity: We collaborate and learn from global worker movements to strengthen labour justice everywhere.
