Cde Sipho Dlamini - Regional Chairperson

Cde Dlamini is a long-time labour activist from KwaZulu-Natal who has served as a shop steward and organiser for over 15 years. His work focuses on strengthening member participation and ensuring fair bargaining across key public and private sectors.

×

Cde Lindiwe Ncube - Regional Secretary

Cde Ncube brings administrative precision and advocacy experience from years of service within education and healthcare sectors. She leads regional coordination, ensuring timely communication and implementation of NDASWU policies.

×

Cde Thabo Molefe - Regional Treasurer

With a background in accounting and finance, Cde Molefe manages regional resources with transparency and accountability, ensuring members’ contributions are utilised effectively for campaigns and welfare.
